A3 Award Logo

2019
graphicgrasshoppercode

This was my entry to the A3 (Annual Architecture Awards) logo competition. The graphic was generated using a reaction-diffusion physics simulation in Grasshopper, which I configured to store the entire time-series of the simulation. By extruding this data with the Z-axis representing time, the final form visualizes the 'A3' letters growing to fill the constraints of the badge shape. The result is an intricate pattern where the content of the logo is determined by the constraints of its graphic container.

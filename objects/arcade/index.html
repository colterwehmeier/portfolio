Arcade Cabinet

Winter, 2022
furnitureconceptwoodtechnologygames

I was interested in CRT displays and tried to design an ideal arcade cabinet for exhibiting contemporary art games in a space like a gallery. The CRT is actually a housing over a flat panel, which also contains the computer. I would want to mill and polish acrylic into a CRT-like curved lens to sit on top of the flat panel screen.

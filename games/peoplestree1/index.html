The Peoples Tree

Winter, 2020
gamemadventhaunted-ps1analyticscitizen-scienceresearchclowder
The People's Tree ↗ was an experiment about community building in networked games. Players design ornaments and leave messages on a shared tree. One key finding from this and subsequent projects like Córdoba Court was an unexpectedly high player return rate of 15-30%. This suggests that even without an explicit goal, players felt a personal stake in the outcome of the collectively-designed artifact and returned to observe its evolution. The app was an opportunity to build technology, policy, and run analytics.

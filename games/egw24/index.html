Adam Pype and I were invited to present C.H.A.I.N.G.E.D. and our work with the Haunted PlayStation 1 collective at the esteemed Experimental Games Workshop (EGW) ↗ during the Game Developers Conference (GDC) in San Francisco. The EGW, a GDC tradition spanning over 20 years, celebrates games at the forefront of gameplay, organization, and creative direction.

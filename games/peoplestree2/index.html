Winter, 2021

The People's Tree project ↗ served as an experimental exploration into community building within networked games. Participants could design ornaments and leave messages on a communal tree, offering a unique blend of interaction and shared digital space. This application provided a valuable opportunity to develop technology, establish policy frameworks, and conduct comprehensive analytics.

Córdoba Court

2022
gamemadventsolosocial experimentcitizen-scienceclowderreportanalyticsgame retrospectivegames
Córdoba Court ↗ is an innovative social game designed to foster community building. Set in a virtual space modeled after the communal patios of Córdoba, Spain, it invites players to cultivate the environment akin to physical community greening and habitation practices. Players express their individuality through the creation of personalized totems, choosing between a potted plant or a ceramic plate, with millions of possible design combinations. A report ↗ is also available, which analyses the content posted and examines some of the behaviors observed within the space.

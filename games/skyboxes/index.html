2010

My earliest experiences with photoshop and art making from ~2008-2010. I used Terragen, Vue, and photocollage to build skyboxes for the source engine. I also got into making 360 panoramic images from my own camera. A friend bundled the materials I had distributed into one package ↗. Skies were made for personal projects, friend's projects, or mods.

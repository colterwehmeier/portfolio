The Austere

2018
architecturecompetitiondesignspeculative designarexhibition
The Austere began as a realistic entry into the Solar Decathlon Design-Build competition, highlighted in the Graze pitch video featuring Alex Brosh and Shannon Fitzgerald. After submitting our pitch, I transformed the design into a speculative fiction piece focusing on degrowth, American cottage-core, and the app-economy. This unique competition-style presentation spread ↗ won the Critical Mass student choice award that year. Subsequently, I adapted it for inclusion in the Guchibytes exhibition as an AR installation, offering viewers a glimpse into the peculiar world suggested by the presentation.

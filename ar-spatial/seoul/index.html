Cyprus Pavilion: Seoul Bienalle

Summer, 2018
exhibitionvrclimatedesignfabrication3d-printinstallation

I assisted the VELab at the Cyprus Institute with a lightweight VR installation. I designed a 3D-printed, stereogram-inspired VR system that loops 360-degree stereo videos. Guests can view various climate modeling scenarios and disasters unfolding within our virtual model of Nicosia.

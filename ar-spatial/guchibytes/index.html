Guchibytes

Fall, 2019
exhibitionzinearchitecturedesignarspeculationfurniturewikar
Built from the remaining pieces of the digital picnic after we recycled them into furniture, guchibytes was an exhibition which sought to tie up loose ends in our studio organization Quipit. We collected unfinished speculative or sarcastic pieces and exhibited them together, creating a print zine ↗ and AR content to accompany each piece.

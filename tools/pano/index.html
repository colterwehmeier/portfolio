360 Visualizer

2023
web tool
Developed a tool for the Cyprus Institute to develop immersive 360 degree panoramas with text and recorded sound for exhibitions. The webapp works in all major browsers and uses device compass/gyro (or click and drag) to rotate the panorama intuitively. A demo can be found here. ↗

Bezeliness Intensifies

2018
exhibitionartdesignnew-media3d printingfurniturefabricationinstallation

This art and research project studies the aesthetics of frames and bezels through the use of space-filling curves, packing algorithms, and reaction-diffusion simulations. It also investigates various digital production techniques, including 3D printing, furniture making, pen plotting, rendering, video production, and reactive lighting for installations.
